Privacy Policy
Last Updated: March 2026

Introduction
MammalList ("we," "us," or "our") is dedicated to the privacy of our users and the safety of the wildlife they document. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Data We Collect
Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username, email address, profile photo, and the other information that you provide to the app.

Sighting Data: We collect photos, text descriptions, species, geolocation and other information that you offer to the app when you submit a sighting.

Usage Data: We collect basic information on how you interact with the app to improve performance.

How We Use Your Data

Community Interaction: Your sightings, text posts, and trip reports are shared with other users based on your privacy settings.

Your Choices

Account Deletion: You may delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.
